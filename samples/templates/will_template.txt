LAST WILL AND TESTAMENT
OF
[TESTATOR FULL NAME]

I, [TESTATOR FULL NAME], currently residing at [TESTATOR FULL ADDRESS],
being of sound mind and disposing memory, and not acting under duress,
menace, fraud, or undue influence of any person, do hereby make,
publish, and declare this instrument to be my Last Will and Testament,
and I hereby revoke any and all prior wills and codicils heretofore
made by me.

ARTICLE I — IDENTIFICATION OF FAMILY

I am [MARITAL STATUS]. [IF MARRIED: My spouse's name is [SPOUSE FULL
NAME].] I have the following children, all of whom are living unless
otherwise stated: [LIST CHILDREN, WITH FULL NAMES AND DATES OF BIRTH].
References in this Will to "my children" shall mean the children
identified in this Article.

ARTICLE II — PAYMENT OF DEBTS AND EXPENSES

I direct my Independent Executor to pay from the residue of my estate
all of my legally enforceable debts, the expenses of my last illness,
my funeral and burial expenses, and the costs of administering my
estate. My Independent Executor shall have discretion to determine the
order and manner of such payments.

    (b) [Repeat as needed.]
]

ARTICLE IV — RESIDUARY ESTATE

All the rest, residue, and remainder of my estate, of whatever kind
and wherever located (my "residuary estate"), I give, devise, and
bequeath as follows:

    [IF MARRIED AND SPOUSE SURVIVES: To my spouse, [SPOUSE FULL NAME],
    if they survive me.

    If my spouse does not survive me, then] to my children in equal
    shares, per stirpes. If any child of mine predeceases me leaving
    no descendants who survive me, that child's share shall pass in
    equal shares to my then-living children, per stirpes.

ARTICLE V — INDEPENDENT EXECUTOR

I appoint [PRIMARY EXECUTOR FULL NAME] as the Independent Executor of
this Will, to serve without bond. If [PRIMARY EXECUTOR FULL NAME] is
unable or unwilling to serve, I appoint [ALTERNATE EXECUTOR FULL NAME]
to serve as Independent Executor, likewise without bond.

It is my intention that my estate be administered as an independent
administration under the provisions of Chapter 401 of the Texas
Estates Code, and that no action be required of or taken in any court
in relation to the settlement of my estate other than the probating
and recording of this Will and the return of any required inventory,
appraisement, and list of claims.

ARTICLE VI — GUARDIAN FOR MINOR CHILDREN

[IF ANY CHILD IS A MINOR: If at my death any of my children is a
minor, I appoint [GUARDIAN FULL NAME] as Guardian of the Person of
such minor child or children. If [GUARDIAN FULL NAME] is unable or
unwilling to serve, I appoint [ALTERNATE GUARDIAN FULL NAME].]

ARTICLE VII — GOVERNING LAW

This Will shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the
laws of the State of Texas.
